User Guide

How to get the most out of Yojana.AI in a few simple steps.

How It Works

Yojana.AI uses a combination of AI-driven eligibility matching and official government data to help Indian citizens discover schemes they may qualify for. The entire process is free, private, and takes under 3 minutes.

1

📋 Fill Your Profile

Enter basic details: age, income, occupation, state, and category. No personal ID is required at this stage.

2

🤖 AI Matching

Our ML engine compares your profile against 50+ government scheme eligibility rules and generates a match score.

3

📊 View Results

See all eligible schemes ranked by success probability. Each card shows required documents and deadlines.

4

📥 Download Guide

Generate a personalized PDF roadmap with step-by-step instructions for applying to your top schemes.

Understanding Your Match Score

Each scheme is assigned a Success Probability (%) based on how closely your profile aligns with the official eligibility criteria. A score above 70% means you are highly likely to qualify, while scores between 40-70% indicate partial eligibility that may require additional verification.

💡 Tip: Update your profile with accurate income and land-holding data to get the most precise scoring. The engine re-ranks results dynamically as you make changes.

Selecting a Language

Yojana.AI supports Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Marathi, and English. Use the language selector in the top-right corner of the results page to switch languages. Scheme names, descriptions, and benefit details will all be translated automatically.

Checking Scheme Details

Click on any scheme card in the results page to open the full detail view. This includes the official scheme summary, applicable ministry, maximum benefit amount, required documents, and a direct link to the official government portal to begin your application.

Downloading the PDF Report

On the results page, click "Download Report". A personalized PDF is generated with your top eligible schemes, a step-by-step application checklist, and key deadline reminders. This feature is available on all plans.
